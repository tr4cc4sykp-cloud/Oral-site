I. INTRODUCTION

"Today, I am going to talk about a movie that has defined a whole generation of car enthusiasts: The Fast and the Furious, released in 2001 and directed by Rob Cohen. I chose this subject because it perfectly connects my interest in cinema with my technical background in the automotive industry. This film is the starting point of a legendary franchise. It is set in the underground world of illegal street racing in Los Angeles. It’s not just a movie about cars; it’s a story about adrenaline, respect, and a very specific lifestyle where people live their lives 'a quarter-mile at a time'."
1:15 - 2:45

II. THE PLOT & CHARACTERS

"The story follows Brian O’Conner, an undercover LAPD officer. His mission is to infiltrate a crew of street racers suspected of hijacking trucks to steal electronic equipment. He meets Dominic Toretto, a charismatic leader who runs a local garage. Brian manages to win Dom’s respect by saving him from the police after a race. The tension comes from Brian’s internal conflict: he becomes part of Dom’s 'family' and falls in love with his sister, Mia. By the end, Brian must choose between his badge and his loyalty. He eventually gives Dom the keys to his car, allowing him to escape."
